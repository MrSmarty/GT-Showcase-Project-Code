About Me

Bio:

Hi, I'm Lincoln. I'm a Sophomore at Lago Vista High School. I have many hobbies and love a good challenge.
I'm a member of the LVHS Programming team, and helped our team to win district this year. Our Region meet is April 22 at Davinport High School.
I play Trumpet in our school's Marching Band, am a multi-instrumentalist in the Jazz Band, and play the Cello with the Central Texas Youth Philharmonic Orchestra. I also enjoy playing Stadium Organ for our Baseball team, and am hoping to start playing for the Basketball team too.
I will sometimes run Tech for the Theater groups in our small town doing Spotlight, Lights, and Sound.
My goal is to go to college at Texas A&M where I wish to get my Masters Degree in Computer Science, march with the Fightin' Texas Aggie Band, and hone my Ping-Pong skills.
